About us

We are Druzhina Putivl, the Washington, DC area chapter of ORUR, a scouting organization serving people of Russian heritage worldwide. We trace our roots to the original Russian scouts founded in 1909.

Our mission is to help raise boys and girls into physically and spritually healthy young people capable of succeeding in today's world, while preseving Russian culture and heritage.
